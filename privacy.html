6. User Data Deletion Instructions

If you wish to delete your data associated with Automation that we have collected through Facebook Login, you have two primary methods:

  1. Through Facebook:

    You can remove our app from your Facebook account, which will revoke our access to your data. To do this:

    • Go to your Facebook "Settings & Privacy" > "Settings".
    • Navigate to "Apps and Websites".
    • Find "Automation" in the list and click "Remove".
    • Follow the prompts to confirm the removal. This will stop our app from receiving new data from Facebook and may trigger a data deletion request to us.
  2. Direct Request to Us:

    You can also send a direct request to us to delete your data. Please contact us at bhardwajnaina50@gmail.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request - Automation". In your email, please include your Facebook User ID (if known) or the email address associated with your Facebook account to help us identify your data. We will process your request in accordance with applicable laws and Facebook's Platform Policies.

Upon receiving a valid data deletion request, we will delete or anonymize your personal data from our active databases within a reasonable timeframe, unless retention is required by law or for legitimate business purposes.
